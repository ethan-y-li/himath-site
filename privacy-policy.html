HiMath Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 18, 2026

HiMath is a math practice application.

Summary

  • HiMath stores study progress and preferences locally on the device using UserDefaults.
  • HiMath does not collect personal data from users.
  • HiMath does not transmit user study data to the developer.
  • HiMath does not share user data with third parties.
  • HiMath does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.
  • HiMath uses Apple StoreKit only for in-app purchases and subscription restoration.

Local Storage

HiMath stores app state such as selected course, streaks, review state, progress, and related study preferences locally on the device. This information is used only so the app can function properly for the user.

Purchases

HiMath uses Apple StoreKit for displaying in-app purchase products, completing purchases, restoring purchases, and checking subscription entitlement status. Purchase processing is handled by Apple.

No Data Collection or Sharing

HiMath does not operate its own user account system, backend sync service, analytics pipeline, advertising SDK, or third-party tracking system. User progress is not collected, transmitted, sold, or shared by the developer.

Changes

This policy may be updated from time to time. The current version will be posted at this URL.
